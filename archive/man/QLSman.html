NAME

QLS − Quickly List HOME and BIN folders

AUTHOR

Copyright (c) 2024 Jacob "Yeetsup" Hache. Licensed under a YSSL 1.0 license: <https://yeetssite.github.io/yssl-1.0/>

SYNOPSIS

qls [ -f | --flag ]

DESCRIPTION

QLS − Quick List Quickly lists important directories such as your home or bin

OPTIONS

−H, −−help

Prints some help

−h, −−home

Lists your home directory

−b, −−bin

Lists your bin directory

−v, −−version

Prints the installed version
